Our Story

A place that we love best

Growing up just an hour apart, Riley and Allen would not meet until the hands of fate (in the form of a summer job) brought them together.

One beautiful midsummer's day at Camp Don Lee, Allen invited Riley to be his date to the famous Croaker Festival. After a night of patriotic fireworks and a long walk on a pier they knew they had something special.

Together they went to Colorado to begin a new adventure.

The first of many.
